Areas most people do not realize fall under networking
Undersea cables
Over 500 submarine cables connect every continent at the ocean floor — they carry ~99% of international data
Satellite networking
Low-earth orbit constellations, delay-tolerant networking, and deep-space communication protocols
IoT & sensor networks
LoRaWAN, Zigbee, Z-Wave — lightweight protocols built specifically for low-power devices and sensors
AI-driven networking
Self-optimizing networks that use machine learning to predict congestion and reroute traffic automatically
Quantum networking
Quantum key distribution and entanglement-based communication — still active research, becoming real
Internet Exchange Points
Physical locations where ISPs and content providers meet to exchange traffic — critical internet infrastructure

No one masters all of it.

Senior engineers at the largest network companies in the world specialize deeply in one or two domains — not all eight. The goal is not to learn everything at once, but to understand the full map clearly enough to know where you want to go deep. Start with the fundamentals, follow what interests you, and the rest will follow.
