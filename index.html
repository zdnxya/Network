Areas most people do not realise belong to networking
Undersea cables
Over 500 submarine cables connect every continent at the ocean floor
Satellite networking
Starlink, delay-tolerant networking, and communication beyond Earth orbit
IoT networking
LoRaWAN, Zigbee, Z-Wave — lightweight protocols built for sensors and machines
AI-driven networking
Networks that monitor, predict, and optimize themselves using machine learning
Quantum networking
Quantum key distribution and entanglement-based communication — active research area
Internet Exchange Points
Physical locations where all ISPs meet and exchange traffic with each other
You do not need to master all of it

Senior engineers at Google and Cisco specialize in one area — not all eight. What matters is understanding the full map first, then choosing where to go deep.
